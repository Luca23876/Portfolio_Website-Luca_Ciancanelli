3D Printed Test UAV

Pushing the Bounds of Additive Manufacturing for UAVs

Project Overview

This UAV was developed to test additive manufacturing techniques for UAVs and to refine my UAV design process. Utilizing Open Vehicle Sketch Platform, an iterative design process was followed to design an airframe to meet a set of aircraft performance requirements. Once the performance requirements were met, Autodesk Fusion 360 was used to model parts, subassemblies, and the complete airframe assembly. With the airframe designed, 3D printed parts were manufactured using a Bambu P1S 3D printer and off-the-shelf components were purchased through RC parts distributors. Finally, the airframe was assembled, final build specs were verified (total mass, center of mass, etc.), and the airframe was tested at a model airfield.
