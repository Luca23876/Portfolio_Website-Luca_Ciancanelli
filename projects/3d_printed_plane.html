3D Printed Test UAV

Pushing the Bounds of Additive Manufacturing for UAVs

Project Overview

This UAV was developed to test the feasibility of producing a lightweight, fully flight-capable fixed-wing aircraft using 3D printing technologies. The design was modeled in Fusion 360, printed in PLA+, and reinforced with carbon rods.

Specifications

  • Wingspan: 1.2m
  • Weight: 850g
  • Motor: 2200KV brushless
  • Battery: 3S LiPo
  • Flight Controller: Custom PCB
