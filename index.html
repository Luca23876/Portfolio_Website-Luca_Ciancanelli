About Me

I'm an electrical engineer actively seeking opportunities in the aerospace industry. I specialize in embedded systems, control design, and avionics integration, with a strong foundation built through hands-on personal projects. I've designed and tested custom flight controllers for fixed-wing UAVs, developed airframes from the ground up, and created specialized techniques for manufacturing UAVs. My approach emphasizes system-level thinking, rapid prototyping, and a deep curiosity for autonomous flight technologies. I'm eager to bring that same drive and technical rigor to a forward-thinking engineering team.
