About Me

I am an electrical engineer actively seeking opportunities in the aerospace industry. I specialize in embedded systems, control design, and avionics integration, with a solid foundation built through hands-on personal projects. I have designed and evaluated custom flight controllers for fixed-wing UAVs, developed airframes from the ground up, and created specialized techniques for manufacturing UAVs. My approach emphasizes system-level thinking, rapid prototyping, and a deep curiosity for aerospace technologies. I am eager to bring that to an aerospace company with a drive towards creating unique and innovative solutions.
